The Problem

Kampala-Mukono Road Under Pressure

The 25km corridor carries over 50,000 vehicles daily, causing severe congestion, accelerated road deterioration, and economic losses. The road, originally designed for lighter traffic, now serves as the primary freight and commuter artery connecting Kampala to eastern Uganda and Kenya.

Proposed Solution

Straddle-Beam Monorail System

An elevated monorail running above the road median, requiring zero additional road surface and providing fully grade-separated mass transit. Based on proven Cairo/INNOVIA 300 technology.

△

Elevated Guideway

Pre-stressed concrete box beam (850mm x 1500mm) supported by Y-shaped reinforced concrete piers at 25-30m intervals. 10m clearance above road for heavy vehicle passage.

⚡

750V DC Power Supply

Third rail integrated into guideway beam underside, with overhead catenary backup. Substations at 2km intervals. Compatible with Uganda's national grid at 11kV distribution.

■

Y-Shaped Concrete Piers

Reinforced concrete columns in the road median. Foundation footprint 2m x 2m. Pre-cast segments for rapid erection with minimal road closure (night-time installation).

★

8 Elevated Stations

Platform screen doors, escalators/lifts, covered waiting areas. Platforms at guideway level (~12m above ground). Designed to NFPA 130 fire/life safety standards.

⚐

Road User Protection

MASH TL-4 crash-rated barriers around all pier bases. Steel pedestrian fencing along the guideway alignment. LED warning signage at all pier locations. Anti-climb mesh at stations.

⇄

CBTC Signalling

Communications-Based Train Control with Automatic Train Operation (ATO GoA3/4). 90-second minimum design headway. Full CCTV coverage, fire suppression, and emergency walkway on guideway.

Station Design (Cairo Model)

⇧

Elevated Platforms

Centre-platform configuration at guideway level (~12m). Platform screen doors matching train door positions. Level boarding for universal access.

☂

Weather Protection

Full canopy coverage with steel-framed tensile membrane roof. Enclosed air-conditioned waiting areas at platform level.

⚙

Vertical Circulation

2x escalators (up/down), 1x panoramic lift (DDA compliant), 1x fire stair per platform end. Tactile paving and audio announcements throughout.

⍑

Ground-Level Integration

Boda-boda parking, matatu/taxi ranks, bicycle storage, and pedestrian plaza at ground level for seamless multimodal interchange.

Recommendation

Based on the aerial survey analysis, 3D corridor reconstruction, multi-criteria options appraisal, and benchmarking against the Cairo Monorail, this proposal recommends that the Government of Uganda, through the Ministry of Works & Transport, commissions a full feasibility study for the Kampala-Mukono Straddle-Beam Monorail System.

Arab Contractors Uganda Limited, with direct experience from the Cairo Monorail project, is uniquely positioned to deliver this infrastructure for the Republic of Uganda.
